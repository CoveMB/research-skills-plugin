# Source methodology audit

Controlled packet only. The visible material says three cities reported lower congestion after adopting mobility dashboards, but it does not provide selection criteria, a comparison group, a baseline window, a statistical model, a confounder review, or a mechanism test.

## What I can verify

- The packet reports an observed before/after pattern for three cities.
- The packet does not provide enough method evidence to support a causal claim.
- The visible notes can support a limited descriptive claim: three cities reported lower congestion after dashboard adoption.

## What remains uncertain

- No method details are available for city selection, baseline construction, or comparison.
- No comparison group is available.
- Confounders and mechanism evidence are unavailable.
- It is unknown whether the reported change came from the dashboard, other policies, broader traffic shifts, measurement changes, or selection effects.

## User verification needed

- Provide the underlying study, data table, method note, or source text.
- Identify whether there was a comparison group, baseline window, model, or confounder review.
- Verify whether the source itself makes only a descriptive claim or a causal claim.
