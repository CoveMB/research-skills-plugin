This file is a stricter pre-appraisal review. It does not establish source
truth, does not appraise source claims, does not activate the fixture, and does
not replace human review.

Scores below mean confidence that the source record is correctly identified and
that the provisional role is safe enough for human appraisal. They do not mean
that the source's claims are correct or that the source has evidentiary weight
for the gold set.

Every scholarly or decoy record remains `not_appraised`. Evidence strength
remains `not_appraised` until a human reviewer checks what the source does and
does not support. Decoys remain non-evidentiary.

Quoted strings are short source-record or source-page excerpts used only to
confirm identity, source type, or decoy status. They are not gold-set evidence.

## Score Rubric

- `5/5`: source identity is stable and the provisional role is straightforward.
- `4/5`: source identity is stable, but appraisal must preserve clear role or
  design limits.
- `3/5`: usable candidate, but prioritize human review because metadata,
  access, language, source type, or evidentiary scope remains fragile.
- `2/5`: materially unresolved; do not rely on the record without more
  reconciliation.
- `1/5`: hold; no stable scholarly source record has been recovered.
- `retired_not_scored`: removed from candidate use; retained only so the
  discarded lead does not re-enter the packet accidentally.
