## Score Rubric

- `5/5`: metadata and source type are stable; role is strongly supported by a
  reliable scholarly or publisher record.
- `4/5`: metadata is stable, but role still needs normal source-appraisal
  caution such as author-side status, preliminary design, or indirect relevance.
- `3/5`: source is usable as a candidate but needs priority human review because
  of language, page/metadata caution, weak design, abstract-only status, or
  secondary/contextual role.
- `2/5`: low confidence; DOI, venue quality, source type, or metadata remains
  materially unresolved.
- `1/5`: hold; no stable scholarly source record has been recovered.
