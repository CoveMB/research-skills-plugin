## Quality gates

Before source discovery:

- Central question is stable enough to guide search.
- Corpus boundaries are explicit.
- Source types are separated by role.

Before argument mapping:

- Primary and secondary sources are separated.
- Opposing literatures and rival explanations are represented.
- Major claims are classified as descriptive, causal, interpretive, theoretical, or normative.

Before chapter design:

- Thesis tree exists.
- Evidence status is attached to major claims.
- Speculative claims are labeled.

Before release review:

- A release artifact exists.
- Citations, quotations, figures, tables, source provenance, privacy exposure, rights status, and AI/human workflow checkpoints are available for audit.
