# Systematic Review Method Audit

## Audit questions

- Is the review question, protocol, databases, search strings, dates, and filters visible?
- Are screening, exclusion reasons, reviewer process, and conflict resolution documented?
- Is there an appraisal plan, risk of bias or quality assessment, and synthesis method?
- Are certainty, confidence, or evidence-strength limits reported where appropriate?
- If a meta-analysis or pooled estimate is claimed, are effect measures, denominators, heterogeneity, model choice, inclusion logic, and sensitivity checks visible?

## Boundaries

Use this reference when a review source is expected to summarize a field, estimate effects, map evidence, or justify consensus claims.

## Cannot support

- Claims of consensus when the search, screening, appraisal, or synthesis process is unavailable.
- Effect estimates from scoping or narrative reviews that did not perform that synthesis.
- Field-complete claims from a review with narrow, stale, or one-sided coverage.
- Informal meta-analysis from incompatible studies, mixed measures, missing denominators, or unreported heterogeneity.
